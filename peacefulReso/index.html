Peaceful Resonance "Peaceful Resonance" is an immersive audiovisual exploration that invites the viewer to discover the subtle connections between sound and visual form. Through the interplay of responsive audio and visuals, the artwork creates a meditative environment where the rhythmic movements of light and the tranquil resonance of sound converge. The seamless integration of these sensory elements evokes a sense of peaceful contemplation, allowing the audience to become fully immersed in the harmonious whole.



Peaceful Resonance, 2024
