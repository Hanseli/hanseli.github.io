ABOUT

Hansel born and raised in Hong Kong, graduated from RMIT’s Fine Art program in 2009 and the University of West England’s Electronic Communication Engineering program in 2023. He explores a variety of creative languages through mediums like painting, sound, and electronics. With over a decade of experience in visual art education, Hansel co-founded BiLiBaLa Art Studio in 2021 and dedicates himself to art creation and education.

李振忠出生並成長於香港，於2009年畢業於皇家墨爾本理工大學的藝術課程，並於2023年畢業於西英格蘭大學的電子通訊工程課程。他透過繪畫、聲音和電子等多種媒介探索各種創作語言。李氏在視覺藝術教育領域有超過十年的經驗，於2021年共同創辦了BiLiBaLa藝術工作室，致力藝術創作及教育的工作。
