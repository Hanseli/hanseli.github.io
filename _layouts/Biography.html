Project



2023 - 2024

Kowloon City Youth Arts Festival 

In order to provide diversified artworks creation trainings for teenagers in Kowloon City District, the Kowloon City Youth Development Network under the Kowloon City District Office will organize the "Kowloon City Youth Arts Festival 2023-2024" from September 2023 to March 2024. The Festival aims to provide a platform for teenagers to showcase their talents by organizing arts exhibitions in the District.

2023 - 2024

Somewhere Over The Lung Tsun Bridge 

As an experienced art teacher, Hanseli has shared their passion and knowledge of art with students of all ages. From teaching foundational techniques to encouraging creative exploration, Hanseli has helped many aspiring artists find their voice and hone their skills.

2019

Playful Public Design

The collaborative design research project between the Public Design Lab at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University's School of Design and the Art Design Education Studio aimed to promote inclusive design.

2015 - 2017

Gashapon

The initial appearance of capsule vending machines pointed to a new (consumer) experience, adding enjoyment to the process through the concept of probability. People make a purchase with the expectation of experiencing the gap between their anticipation and the actual result.

2016

Inclusive Playground

As part of the "Inclusive Playground Design" held in collaboration with Creativekids and Playright Children's Play Association, a series of courses were designed for children to learn and actively participate in the design of inclusive playgrounds. 

2016

Ednovation Festival

To broaden children's imagination about chairs and explore different methods of constructing sturdy structures, a unique chair was designed using only cardboard paper. This project aimed to inspire children how to create functional furniture with their own hands. In November 2016, during the Ednovation Fest, personalized chair designs by children were showcased, featuring life-sized chairs made from rigid cardboard.
